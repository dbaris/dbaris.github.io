WOVEN STOOL

Made from baltic birch plywood and 1/4" steel rod, the woven stool embraces merging digital fabrication with analog making technologies. Though the pieces are fabricated using the CNC and KUKA rod bender, assembly requires hand weaving the rods together and welding them into place on a steel plate. The stool includes a built-in shelf to store materials such as books, magazines, or papers.
