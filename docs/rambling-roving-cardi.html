RAMBLING ROVING CARDIGAN

Rambling Roving is a woolly and whimsical winter cardigan. Worked in raglan construction, the cardigan is worn with a shrug fit. Bubble sleeves add volume to the arms and cinch at the wrist for mobility. Textural patterning is explored through the use of single-ply bulky yarn, color-blocking, and a two-color brioche stitch hem. The result is a unique, custom garment that evokes a pastoral landscape.
