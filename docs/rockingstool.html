ROCKING STOOL

Fabricated from a 2'x 4' sheet of 3/4" baltic birch plywood, this rocking stool is a study in multipurpose digital fabrication and design using the CNC. The stool flips between two positions: a gentle rocking stool with an elliptical base and a flat-bottomed side table. Carefully placed cutouts allow this lightweight stool to be easily transported.
