Daydream Cardigan

The Daydream Cardigan is a woolly and whimsical raglan cardigan with a shrug fit. It plays boldly with texture through bulky yarn, bubble sleeves, color-blocking, and a two-color brioche hem. In these difficult times, it’s more important than ever to take a little time to daydream, investing in our collective imagination for a better and more beautiful world.

Rosa Tank Top

The Rosa Tank is a 90’s inspired fitted summer tank featuring a high neckline, triangular lacework, and I-Cord finishing. The construction is knit from the top down and works up quickly. Wear it as it is for a gorgeous warm-weather tank, crop it shorter to make a bralette, or layer it with a cute cardigan for cooler seasons. The lace section at the bottom can be omitted for beginner knitters or true committed minimalists.
